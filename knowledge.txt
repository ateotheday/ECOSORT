EcoSort is an AI-powered waste classification system developed by students of College of Engineering Munnar.

Team members:
- Aathira R N
- Aleena Ajith
- Ameen PP
- Thabsheera Thasnim

Guided by:
- Dr. Soumya
- HOD CSE Dr. Deepa S Kumar

It classifies waste into two categories:
- Organic
- Recyclable

The system uses a YOLO model trained on the TACO dataset.

Users can:
- Upload waste images
- Get classification results
- Use hardware-based sorting with ESP32 and servo motors
- View leaderboard rankings

Without login:
- Users can classify waste
- View leaderboard

With login:
- Users can use hardware system
- Earn points

A research paper was published in IJMETS April edition.
